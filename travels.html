Southwest Airlines

Southwest is a popular airline in my family. It flies out of my two local airports - BUR and LAX. Every hour, it flies to the Bay Area, where we have family and my daughters went to college.

Hack #1: Book your flight, then check the pricing regularly.

https://thepointsguy.com/guide/reprice-southwest-flight-decrease/

Hack #2: Wanna Get Away Plus

Credits can be transferred to anyone with a Rapid Rewarda account, not just for you.

You can also make confirmed same-day flight changes without paying a fee. This is not only nice to get on an earlier or later flight if you want, it can be used to get you the flight you really want, if you're up for the risk. Just book the cheapest flight for that day - these are usually really early or late in the day. Then, at 12:00:01 after midnight, log on to Southwest.com and change the flight to the one you really want. But, if your ideal flight is booked, be prepared to get to the airport at the crack of dawn!
