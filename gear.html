Travel Hardware

AirTags

AirTags have rescued my family multiple times since they first came out. I keep them in our luggage and on my wallet. On a recent trip to New Orleans, I left my bag in the taxi from the airport. With AirTags, I was able to see where the cab was as I frantically called to get my stuff back. In Ethiopia, the airline insisted my luggage was still at Heathrow and would arrive the next day. I persuaded them that my bag was, in fact, in the terminal and we could go get it at that moment. Buy a 4-Pack of AirTags

Anker Portable Charger 525

This portable charger is a little bigger than a Max-sized iPhone and weighs a little more than the phone. While smaller would be nicer, the power makes it worthwhile. It can charge an iPhone 12 to 50% in 30 minutes. It will fully charge a phone several times - it claims 5, but I've never tried. Someone in my family is almost out of battery when we travel, so it's a life saver, too. Buy the Anker 525 Portable Charger

Etekcity Luggage Scale 2-Pack

Right before international trips, my house is aflame with people worried their suitcases are overweight. And, they usually are. This two-pack of digital scales are accurate and inexpensive enough to buy two. So, more family members can weigh and pack at the same time! You can also pack one with you so all the newly acquired items from your trip don't lead to overweight bag fees. Buy the Anker 525 Portable Charger

Gear AID HEROCLIP Carabiner (Medium)

More than one person has told me it looks like I have handcuffs on my backpack. It's just this carabiner that lives on my backpack. I didn't know I needed this until I had one. It unfolds into a hook, which you can hook on the inside of public bathroom stalls, on your chair in a restaurant, or out of the way in your hotel room. You can hook your water bottle to it Buy the HEROCLIP carabiner

EPICKA Universal Travel Adapter

This adapter works in every country I've brought it to so far. It's got 4 USB ports for charging multiple things at once. And one USB-C for good measure. It's not a voltage converter, but I don't need that most of the time. Buy this universal travel adapter

MZOO Sleep Eye Mask

This mask stays dark much better than a flat mask. And the memory foam around the eyes means the mask doesn't rub against my eyes. It's light and washable. Buy this Sleep Mask

Morfone 16 Pack Travel Bottles Set for Toiletries

I carry my lotion, toothpaste, soap, shaving cream and more in these silicone bottles and jars. Great for shorter trips of five days or less for me. Buy these reusable silicone travel bottles

BAGAIL Compression Packing Cubes

I'm a convert to compression packing cubes. They make the inside of my suitcase or bag so much more organized. When all the pants are in one cube and socks and underwear in another, I can grab exactly what I need without messing everything else up. These ones feel more solid that other ones we have in the family. Buy compression packing cubes

Bose Noise Cancelling Headphones 700

I picked up these noise-cancelling headphones during Black Friday deals. My ear canals get irritated from AirPods and these Bose headphones have dramatically better noise-cancelling capabilities. They are very comfortable and have great audio sound. They have a wire jack and fit into the audio jack on the planes that I've flown in so far. The headphones have a companion phone application allows you to control the level of noise cancellation. I usually put the headphones on mid-level noise cancellation during phone calls, but at 100% in flight or when trying to sleep in a noisy place. Buy Bose noise-cancelling headphones

Peak Design Mobile Tripod

I balked a little at the price at first, but when it arrived, it feels like a precise instrument - it is not flimsy at all. It's magnetic, so it sticks to the back of newer iPhones. No more holding your hands out in the air for selfies, no more shaky pictures during zooming. It's a tiny tripod that also makes a great stand.

The downside is it makes your phone really heavy because it's so solid. I also have to remove it to put my phone in my car's charger dock. Because it's the size of a credit card, I'm afraid I'll lose it. So far, I'm been careful - and lucky. Buy the Peak Design mobile tripod.
